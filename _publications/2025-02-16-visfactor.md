## Abstract

This paper introduces VisFactor, a novel benchmark designed to evaluate the fundamental visual cognition capabilities of Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs). Our benchmark is based on the Kit of Factor-Referenced Cognitive Tests, which provides a systematic framework for assessing various cognitive abilities. Through comprehensive evaluation, we identify current challenges faced by MLLMs in visual reasoning tasks and analyze the underlying factors that contribute to their limitations.

## Key Contributions

- Development of a comprehensive benchmark for evaluating visual cognition in MLLMs
- Systematic analysis of current MLLM capabilities and limitations
- Identification of key factors affecting visual reasoning performance
- Insights into future directions for improving multimodal AI systems
